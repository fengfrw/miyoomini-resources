Rescue firmware:
1. The extracted img file,
2. Use tools to flash img to a blank TF card,
3. Insert the pre written TF card back into A30,
5. Connect the power, turn on the machine,
6. After automatic completion, shut down and retrieve the card,
7. Check if it starts up normally,
8. Perform firmware upgrade again.

Attention: During flashing, a splash screen will appear, which is normal!

救砖固件：
1、解压得到img文件，
2、使用工具（rufus）刷写到空白TF卡，
3、刷写好的TF卡插回A30机器，
5、插电开机自动刷机，
6、完成后关机取卡，
7、能正常开机完成，
8、重新进行正常固件升级。

注意：过程期间会出现花屏现象属正常！
